Leading the Way in Sustainable E-waste and Li-ion Battery Recycling

Remine India is a recognized startup dedicated to promoting sustainable and responsible recycling of electronic waste (e-waste) and Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. Our mission is to create a cleaner and greener future by minimizing the negative impact of e-waste on the environment. We are committed to using the latest technologies and processes to provide safe, efficient, and cost-effective e-waste recycling solutions. At Remine India, we believe that recycling is not just a responsibility, but a way of life. Join us in our mission to make the world a better place.

Our Services

Our services include responsible e-waste and Li-ion battery recycling, as well as secure data destruction to protect your privacy.

E-waste Recycling

Our certified recycling facility offers responsible and secure e-waste recycling services, ensuring that electronic devices are properly disposed of and recycled in a sustainable manner.

Li-ion Battery Recycling

We provide safe and efficient Li-ion battery recycling solutions, minimizing the negative impact of discarded batteries on the environment.


Data Destruction

Our data destruction service ensures that all sensitive and confidential data is permanently erased from your electronic devices before recycling, providing peace of mind and protection of your privacy
